About Taking Exams at Recipro Skills

"Degrees may open doors, but Skills keep them Open!"

After clicking "Take Exam", you will be redirected to the exam form (Gform) where you can answer the questions. You will need to provide necessary details like your name and email to start the exam.

Marking Scheme: +2 for each correct answer and 0 for incorrect or unattempted answers.
Important: This is a One or More Than One Correct Option exam. If you leave even a single correct option unanswered, the whole answer will be considered wrong.

You can attempt the exam as many times as you like. However, to pass the exam, you must score at least 60%.

After submitting your answers, your response will be processed and stored in our system. You can view your scores immediately after submission.

If you score 60% or more, you will receive your certificate via email (within 5 minutes of submission).
