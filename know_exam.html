About Taking Exams at Recipro Skills

"Degrees may open doors, but Skills keep them Open!"

Important: Exams are conducted twice a year, and you can attend only once per session. The exam fee is Rs.350, and registration dates will be announced periodically.

Exam Process

1 Registration & Confirmation

After registration and payment, you'll receive a confirmation email within 3 business days.

2 Exam Date Assignment

You'll receive your specific exam date after registration closes. The exam can be taken any time during your allotted date.

3 Exam Access

You'll receive the exam link 2 days before your scheduled date. Access is restricted to your registered email only.

4 Exam Format

Questions may have multiple correct options. All correct options must be selected for the question to be marked right.

5 Results & Certification

Results are instant. Passing candidates receive certificates within 5 minutes via email. Failed candidates can reappear in the next session.
