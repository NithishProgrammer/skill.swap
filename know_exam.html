About Taking Exams at Recipro Skills

"Degrees may open doors, but Skills keep them Open!"

The exams will be conducted twice a year, and a person can attend only once per session. To appear for the next session, you need to pay when the registration dates are announced.

The exams cost Rs.350. After making the payment, you will receive further instructions. The exam dates will be announced seperately for you through mailing you at your registered mail, and you will only be able to write the exam on the announced date only.

If you don't receive an email within three business days after registering, please send a complaint email to info.reciproskills@gmail.com with the following details:

  • Your name
  • Your registered email
  • Payment Receipt PDF (sent after payment)

Process

  • The exams will be online. After you register you will get a Confirmation email.

  • You will get your date for your examination seperately after the registration for the exams fully closes, only in that date you will be able to access the exam. You can write the exam whenever You want to in your alloted date

  • On the day of the exam (or 2 days before exam date), You will receive an email with the link to the exam. You would be able to access the exam with the registered email only

  • The exam is one or more than one correct option based. Even you leave one correct option blank, the whole question would be wrong. So be careful and analyse the questions Properly
  • After the exam, you will receive the results within seconds. If you pass, you will receive a certificate within 5 minutes in your mail. If you fail, you can reappear in the next session that year

If you have any questions , Queries, or need assistance, feel free to reach out to our Support Team . We are here to help you!
