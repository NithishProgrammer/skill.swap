Users/Teachers Terms

Everything you need to know about using Recipro Skills & Terms

Welcome to Recipro Skills

Recipro Skills is a platform designed to connect like-minded people to share knowledge and learn together. Whether you are a teacher creating a course or a user enrolling, this guide will help you understand how it works.

ReciproSkills is a user-driven, peer-to-peer learning platform where learners swap skills instead of paying for courses. Users teach what they know and learn what they need, creating an accessible, community-driven education system.

For Using

When you register for a course, your details will be shared with the respective course maker. Please note that acceptance into the course purely depends on the course maker

If you are not accepted, you can try again with another course or instructor. You can communicate with the course maker through mail / other platform where both feels okay if they accepted the swap and head over to Recipro Skills to Connect at the time of Swapping and Learning.

For Teaching

We have some certain pre-defined syllabus for "Certain / Some Course topics". We suggest you to follow those syllabus for teaching that would best align with our exam syllabus and make the learning even more beneficial. If you are creating a course under those topics, we suggest to follow our syllabus for better benefit. View Syllabus for Topics

Swapping & Learning

Swappers/Instructors can use any platform to connect, teach, and learn. Recommended: Our VidRooms, available through your Dashboard.

Course Conduct

Course makers must provide details including course topics, timing, and join link or VidRoom conference number if accepted. Failure to do so may result in complaints and account suspension.

Safety & Reporting

If a user misbehaves, you are free to report them, and necessary action will be taken. For serious issues such as online abuse, file a complaint through our portal with proof or report to nearby authorities.

Our Mission

At Recipro Skills, we aim to connect people with similar interests, enabling them to swap knowledge and learn. Together, we create a dynamic and interactive learning environment.

Create, Teach, Learn, at one place (Recipro Skills)
