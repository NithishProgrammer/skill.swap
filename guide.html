Users/Teachers Guide

Everything you need to know about using Recipro Skills

Welcome to Recipro Skills

Recipro Skills is a platform designed to connect like-minded people to share knowledge and learn together. Whether you are a teacher creating a course or a user enrolling, this guide will help you understand how it works.

For Users

When you register for a course, your details will be shared with the respective course maker. Please note that acceptance into the course purely depends on the course maker.

For Teachers

As a course maker, you will receive an email from "Form Submit" when your first attendee registers for your course. To ensure smooth communication, activate your form using the link in the email. Once activated, you'll receive emails whenever someone registers for your course.

Course Conduct

Courses can be conducted on any authorized online platform such as Google Meet, Zoom, Microsoft Teams, or similar tools. Please make sure to provide the required meeting details to your attendees in a timely manner.

Our Mission

At Recipro Skills, we aim to connect people with similar interests, enabling them to swap knowledge and learn. Together, we create a dynamic and interactive learning environment.

Note: All communication between users and course makers is monitored to ensure safety and transparency. If you face any issues, feel free to reach out.
